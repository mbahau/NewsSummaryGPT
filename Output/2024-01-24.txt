News of 24 January 2024

 * India is set to celebrate its 75th year of independence on January 26, 2024.
* The Prime Minister Narendra Modi has announced that the government will set up a tribunal to decide on the compensation for farmers who were affected by the implementation of the three farm laws.
* The Union Home Ministry has issued an order banning the entry of foreign nationals into India without prior permission from the government.
* The Delhi Police has arrested two men for allegedly raping a child in a park in south Delhi.
* A group of farmers in Punjab has announced that they will boycott the upcoming Lok Sabha elections if the central government does not withdraw the three farm laws.
* The Prime Minister Narendra Modi has invited the President of South Korea, Moon Jae-in, to visit India in March 2023 to boost bilateral ties between the two countries.
* The Central Bureau of Investigation (CBI) has launched a preliminary enquiry into allegations of irregularities and corruption in the allocation of funds for the construction of the Central Vigilance Commission (CVC) building in New Delhi.
* The government has announced that it will provide financial assistance to the families of farmers who have died due to suicide.
* The Prime Minister Narendra Modi has announced that India will celebrate its 75th year of independence by organizing a series of events and activities across the country, including a grand parade in New Delhi on January 26, 2024.
