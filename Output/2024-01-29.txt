News of 29 January 2024

 * A fire broke out at the Bagalkot district police station in Karnataka, causing extensive damage to the building and killing three policemen.
* In the aftermath of the incident, there have been reports of a possible plot to assassinate Karnataka Chief Minister Basavaraj Bommai.
* The Indian Navy's INS Sumitra responded to a distress call regarding hijacking of an Iranian-flagged fishing vessel in Somalia's Gulf of Aden, ensuring the safe release of all crew members and the boat.
* The main opposition MDP in the Maldives is planning to submit a motion to impeach President Mohamed Muizzu after clashes broke out in Parliament on Sunday between pro-government MPs and opposition lawmakers over differences over the approval of four members of the pro-China president's cabinet.
* In Georgia, a 25-year-old Indian student was hammered to death by a homeless drug addict whom he had been helping for the past couple of days.
