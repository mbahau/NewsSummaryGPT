News of 20 January 2024

 * India and China are likely to hold talks on Wednesday to resolve their border dispute, with foreign ministry officials set to meet in Nayagiri, India.
* The United States is reportedly considering deploying additional troops to the Middle East to counter Iran's alleged nuclear program, according to sources.
* The Israeli military has announced that it will continue its offensive against Hamas in Gaza for the time being, with no end in sight to the conflict.
* Russia is expected to launch an attack on Ukraine in the coming days or weeks, according to US intelligence reports.
* China has accused the United States of interfering in its internal affairs after President Xi Jinping was named to a second term as head of state and military.
