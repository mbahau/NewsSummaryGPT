News of 26 January 2024

 * Republic Day Parade will be held on January 26, 2024 and will be presided over by President Droupadi Murmu.
* The theme for this year's parade is "Azaad-e-Johdoo" and the slogan is "Har Ghar Tiranga".
* This is the first time that a female president is presiding over the Republic Day Parade.
* The parade will feature a grand tableau showcasing India's rich cultural heritage and its achievements in various fields.
* Daredevil motorcycle stunts, flypast by fighter jets, and helicopter displays are expected to be the highlights of the parade.
* Various state-level cultural programs, traditional dances, and music performances will also be held as part of the Republic Day celebrations.
