News of 31 January 2024


* The US Department of Justice has announced that it will be indicting Chinese state-owned enterprises for cyber espionage and intellectual property theft, including Huawei.
* China's Foreign Ministry has condemned the move, calling it politically motivated.
* The US has accused China of stealing trade secrets from American companies, such as Cisco Systems, Westinghouse Electric Company, and United Technologies.
* The indictments come after President Biden signed an executive order in January that banned Huawei equipment from US networks.
* The Chinese government has also been accused of economic espionage against US companies, including Tesla and Boeing.
* The US has also accused China of hacking into American infrastructure in preparation for potential cyber attacks.
* The announcement of the indictments has led to increased tensions between the US and China, with both sides accusing the other of stealing intellectual property and engaging in cyber espionage.
