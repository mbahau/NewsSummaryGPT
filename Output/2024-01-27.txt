News of 27 January 2024

 Netflix is set to release a number of new and exciting original series in January 2024. Here are some of the highlights:

* "Fool Me Once" - A psychological thriller about a woman who becomes obsessed with investigating her husband's past after he dies suddenly in an accident.
* "Bitconned" - An investigative documentary that exposes crypto scams and fraud in the world of cryptocurrency.
* "Society of the Dead" - A horror movie about a group of survivors who must band together to survive a zombie apocalypse.
* "The Last One" - A drama series that follows the journey of a man and woman as they navigate their relationship in the wake of a global pandemic.
* "Escape from Paradise" - An action thriller about a group of tourists who become trapped on a cruise ship when it is hijacked by terrorists.
* "The Girl with All the Gifts" - A zombie movie that follows the journey of a young girl and her father as they try to survive in a world overrun by undead creatures.
