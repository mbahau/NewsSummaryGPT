News of 21 January 2024

 * A BJP leader named Sudhanshu Trivedi has been elected as MLA from Khandelwal Assembly constituency.
* Another BJP leader, RPN Singh, has been elected as MLA from Khandelwal Assembly constituency.
* The BJP has won 20 out of the 21 seats in the Karnataka Assembly elections.
* The Congress had won only one seat in the election.
* The BJP has formed a government with an absolute majority in Karnataka for the fourth consecutive term.
* Chief Minister Basavaraji Jaitley and Prime Minister Narendra Modi have hailed the BJP's victory.
* The BJP had promised to implement various development projects in the state during its tenure.
