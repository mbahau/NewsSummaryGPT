News of 23 January 2024

 * A statue of Kannada poet and freedom fighter Kuvempu has been unveiled at a park in Bangalore, Karnataka.
* The statue is a replica of a life-size bronze sculpture of Kuvempu and has been made by artist M. S. Nagaraj.
* The statue was unveiled by the Chief Minister of Karnataka, Basavaraj Bommai.
* The park where the statue is located is called Kuvempu Park.
* The total cost of the project, which includes the statue and the park, was Rs 11.05 crore.
