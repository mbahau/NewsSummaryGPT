News of 30 January 2024

- The Assam Assembly is set to be dissolved on March 20 for elections.
- BJP's Karpoori Thakur, who was elected from Barpeta district, was appointed as the Speaker of Assam Assembly.
- The new speaker has pledged to ensure smooth conduct of the assembly proceedings and prevent any disruptions.
- The BJP government in Assam is currently facing criticism over the Citizenship Amendment Act (CAA) and the National Register of Citizens (NRC).
- The CAA aims to define citizenship based on religious identity, while the NRC aims to identify all residents as either Indian or foreigner.
